About Us

We are an independent non-governmental organisation(NGO) consisting of individual local community volunteers who are committed to support refugees and migrants within Donegal. We promote independence and social integration. We are funded by various organisations including the Community Development Programme. To support and bring awareness within our community we hold regular meetings,workshops,trainings and events.
